Instructions for codenames

Please read them carefully


This game is played by two human players (you and another person).

In each round, you either play as a DICTATOR or as an OBSERVER. The dictator decides how to share 100 ECU (Experimental Currency). 100 ECU are equal to 0.05 USD. The observer simply observes the decision by the dictator and payoff for both players realize accordingly.

The game is repeated a certain number of rounds, and each round roles are randomly assigned.

If you understood the instructions correctly press the "DONE" button to proceed to the game.
